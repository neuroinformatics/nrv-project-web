Study on the learning algorithms for neural data analysis

Andrej Cichocki

Laboratory for Advanced Brain Signal Processing, BSI, RIKEN

e-mail: cia@brain.riken.go.jp

Our objective is to develop and apply novel neural networks and blind signal processing algorithms and methods: Spatiotemporal decorrelation, Dynamic Independent Components Analysis (ICA), Multichannel Blind Deconvolution/Separation (MBD), Higher Order Statistics (HOS), Unsupervised Nonlinear Adaptive Filtering (UNAF) and Time-Frequency (TF) methods to analyze multisensory biomedical signals especially EKG/MEG, EMG data.
